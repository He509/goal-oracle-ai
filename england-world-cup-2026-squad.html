🤖 Tuchel's Reasoning

"From day one we were very clear that we are trying to select and build the best possible team, which is not necessarily to select and collect the 26 most talented players. Teams win championships, it is as simple as that."

❓ England World Cup Squad FAQ

Why are Foden and Palmer not in the squad?
Tuchel cited form, team chemistry and trust. Foden's patchy City season and Palmer's drought (no goals or assists in last 7 PL games) saw both dropped in favour of Eze, Rogers and Bellingham.
Why was Maguire left out?
Quansah preferred for his pace and Tuchel's familiarity from Leverkusen scouting. Maguire said he is "shocked and gutted" by the decision despite a strong United season.
Why Toney over DCL or Gibbs-White?
32 goals in 32 Al-Ahli games, elite penalty record, and adaptation to hot weather. With the tournament in summer in North America, Tuchel valued Toney's experience in extreme heat.
When does England play their first match?
17 June 2026 vs Croatia. Followed by Ghana (23 June) and Panama (27 June). The squad gathers in Florida for pre-tournament camp.
Can England win the World Cup?
At odds of 7.50, England are the 3rd favourites behind France (5.50) and Spain (6.00). Captain Harry Kane has called this the "best squad we've ever had". Tuchel's tournament management at club level (Champions League winner with Chelsea) is a major asset.
