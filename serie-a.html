Frequently Asked Questions

Why do Serie A matches have lower goals?

Serie A averages 2.4 goals/game — the lowest of Europe's top 5 leagues. Our Serie A AI tips factor this into Under 2.5 picks more often.

What is Serie A xG and why is it important?

Expected Goals (xG) measures shot quality — not just quantity. Serie A xG analysis reveals teams that are unlucky or overperforming, helping spot value bets.
