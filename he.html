xG Guide
What is xG and why it predicts results better than the scoreline
Goal Oracle AI  ·  5 min read  ·  Apr 8, 2026
Most bettors look at goals scored. Smart money looks at Expected שערים (xG) — the metric that shows what the scoreline should have been.
What is xG?
Expected שערים (xG) assigns a probability to each shot based on its quality. A shot from 6 yards, central, with a clear sight of goal might have an xG of 0.75 — meaning it should result in a goal 75% of the time. A 30-yard speculative effort might be 0.02.
A team can win 2-1 but have an xG of 0.8 vs 2.4 — meaning they were actually outplayed and got lucky. This is information the scoreline hides completely.
